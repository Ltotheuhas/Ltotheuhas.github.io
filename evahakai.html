Neko

Eva Hakai was first established on May 26th 2020 and then set into stone and announced with its original member list on June 17th.

The team was composed of Avenade, Nanoray, Neupink, Murrumur, Zaphyre, Dylee Dee, Nobodyis and Glen Cove.

After that point, Eva Hakai has used its platform to produce cassette tapes for three of the members' most recent albums.

On October 9th, Eva Hakai released its first compilation album "Eva Vol. 1", featuring previously unreleased tracks from each of the members.

On November 18th, Eva Hakai has merged itself into the music label and multimedia art collective No Agreements.

Since then most of the members have continued their operations under that label, while a few others have split up from the project and continued either independently or under a different group.
